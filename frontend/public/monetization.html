LogicTrade — Monetization Stack

This static HTML page documents the live integrations deployed via GitHub → Render at www.logictrade.site.

1. Google AdSense

Display ads for free-tier users. Premium and trial users see no ads.

Configure: NEXT_PUBLIC_AD_CLIENT, AD_SLOT_* env vars + /ads.txt

2. Amazon Associates (Affiliate)

Recommended trading books — we earn from qualifying purchases.

  • Trading in the Zone
  • Technical Analysis of Financial Markets
  • Market Wizards

Configure: AMAZON_AFFILIATE_TAG / NEXT_PUBLIC_AMAZON_AFFILIATE_TAG

3. Razorpay — 7-Day Trial → ₹999/month

New users get a 1-week free Premium trial. After trial, subscribe at ₹999/month.

Configure: RAZORPAY_KEY_ID, RAZORPAY_PLAN_ID, webhook at /api/payments/webhook
